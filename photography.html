Technology

For the photography section of the website, I was placed into a group of three. We came up with the theme of technology for our portfolio which I took to interpret the transition amidst architectural technology and the evolution of architecture all over Manchester.

Photography Portfolio- Technology

Roman Mamucium

An image of the Roman Mamucium in Deansgate-Castlefield, which shows the architecture and building technique of an ancient roman civilization circa 79 AD. The older foundations represent one of the original steps in technological evolution within architecture. The Romans built the Mamucium with military intentions as a fort on naturally defensible sandstone bluff.

Deansgate Bridge

This image of the bridge in Deansgate over ridgewater canal carries a more industrial flair to it. With the pillars and structures below the bridge represent a steampunk like aspect present in many early industrial buildings with the metallic materials showing the transition into a more industrial age of architecture within British construction.

The Green Chimnees

A more modern aesthetic behind this building found next to the deansgate bridge with the use of various windows providing the building with exterior lighting This transition from the more industrial images shows a more colorful building pattern with the green chimnees standing out amidst the building.

Back Turner Street

A similarly more modern brick layout of a narrow alleyway found in Manchester's Northern Quarter. This area however doesnt express the vibrant colors of the previous image as it carries a different tone due to the socio-economic location for it. The vibrancy is instead brought in by some lovely street art seen in the background which stands out among the image.

Deansgate Square

A view of Deansgate square and the four towering skyscrapers currently under construction there. This represents the highest level of socio-economic architecture whilst holding to modern aesthetics and commonalities such as the large overuse of windows which coat the entire building.
